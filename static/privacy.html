ShweChat - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use ShweChat with TikTok login, we collect the following:

  • Your TikTok public profile (username, avatar, and user ID)
  • Your video list (if you grant permission)
  • Your basic activity logs (for analytics)

2. How We Use the Information

  • To personalize your chat experience
  • To provide you with chatbot-based services
  • To improve system performance and detect abuse

3. Data Retention

We retain access tokens and logs temporarily on secure servers. All tokens are stored securely and auto-expire based on TikTok API limits.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third party unless legally required.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data by emailing us at support@mmofficial.asia.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.

If you have concerns, contact us at support@mmofficial.asia.
