ShweChat – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use ShweChat with TikTok login, we may collect:

  • Your TikTok public profile (username, avatar, user ID)
  • Your public video list (if you grant permission)
  • Your activity logs for analytics (non-identifiable)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize your chatbot experience
  • To provide AI-powered assistant services such as marketing, real estate, and live sales tools
  • To improve performance, detect abuse, and enhance user experience

3. Data Retention

We only store data temporarily on secure servers. Tokens and logs are retained in accordance with TikTok’s token expiration policies. All sensitive data is automatically deleted after session expiry.

4. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption, token hashing, and strict access control to ensure your data remains private and secure. Our system monitors for suspicious activity 24/7.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use services like Supabase (for secure database storage), Render (for hosting), and Hugging Face (for AI chat). These services follow GDPR and platform-compliant privacy policies. We never share your data with unauthorized third parties.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information. Your data is only accessible to authorized systems necessary for service delivery. We may disclose data only if legally required by law enforcement or to comply with legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

You can request to delete your data at any time. Contact us at info@ygnb2b.com to initiate removal.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. All changes will be posted on this page with the new effective date.

If you have concerns or questions, please email info@ygnb2b.com.
